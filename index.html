At North West Indoor Golf, YOU can enjoy YOUR VIRTUAL GOLF EXPERIENCE today.

The Facility

We have 2 state-of-the-art golf studios, air conditioned to just the correct temperature so the weather won’t interfere with your game.

Both equipped with top of the range Trackman 4 Launch Monitors, measuring over 50 Golf Club and Golf Ball data parameters measured to ensure the utmost accuracy in simulation.

Trackman Virtual Golf 2 with over 130 iconic golf destinations and major tournament venues including; Valderrama, Gleneagles, Innisbrook Copperhead, and more!

Realistic hitting surface with Tee-Turf. No more rubber driving range tees and rock-hard mats, Tee-Turf provides authentic strike and the ability to use a real golf tee just as you would on the golf course.

No Clubs? No Problem! We have premium loan clubs available at no extra cost, and we only provide use of premium golf balls.

Academy

We have 2 fully qualified PGA Professionals at North West Indoor Golf. There will always be a PGA Professional on site should you find yourself struggling with your game.

We use the latest technology including our Trackman 4 launch monitor and Boditrak pressure mat linked through high speed video to analyse the swing in precise detail.

We can also archive this data and share with you upon completion of your session, either as a refresher or so you can track your improvement.

Paul Woodcock (PGA Head Professional)

Paul is a PGA Qualified Golf Coach, TPI Certified Instructor and Golfsmith Master Club-fitter. With over 10 years of experience Paul is an experienced coach, he is also a European/World Long Drive Athlete with unrivalled knowledge in distance optimisation and power development.

Joe Joiner (Assistant PGA Professional)

With 10 years coaching experience and over 20 years playing experience Joe’s motive for teaching is to help all levels of golfer understand their game & help them to improve their consistency.

Joe’s golfing journey started at 5 years old & being fortunate enough to have received great junior coaching his passion now lies in the development of junior golf, running development programmes to get kids into golf, develop their understanding & enjoyment of the game.

Custom Fitting

Here at North West Indoor Golf we take your game seriously. Our no obligation fitting service will help you dial your equipment to maximise your performance and ultimately, your enjoyment.

Paul is a Master Club Fitter with 15 years of experience in the industry helping golfers of all abilities including golf professionals to achieve that competitive edge.

We stock the best brands in the industry with extensive trial products which can be analysed on our state-of-the-art Trackman 4 Launch Monitor.

Fittings are available from as little as £25 ranging from irons, woods, wedges, putters all the way up to a comprehensive full bag fitting.

At the end of your session you will have a complete recommendation of golf equipment with the data to back it up sent directly to your inbox, and should you wish to proceed and order your new clubs you can be assured our prices are always competitive with even the biggest online golf retailers.
