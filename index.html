Established in 2020 we are offering a state-of-the-art virtual golf centre based in Warrington, the heart of the North West.

Whether it is;

  • Independent practice time with all the data considered essential by the elite tour professionals.
  • A place to meet up with friends to play some of the best golf courses in the world in as little as 90 minutes.
  • Your way to get your golf fix regardless of the weather.

At North West Indoor Golf, YOU can enjoy YOUR VIRTUAL GOLF EXPERIENCE today.

The Facility

We have a state-of-the-art golf studio, air conditioned to just the correct temperature so the weather won’t interfere with your game.

Top of the range Trackman 4 Launch Monitor with over 50 Golf Club and Golf Ball data parameters measured to ensure the utmost accuracy in simulation.

Trackman Virtual Golf 2 with over 100 iconic golf destinations and major tournament venues including; Valderrama, Gleneagles, Innisbrook Copperhead, and more!

Realistic hitting surface with Tee-Turf. No more rubber driving range tees and rock-hard mats, Tee-Turf provides authentic strike and the ability to use a real golf tee just as you would on the golf course.

No Clubs? No Problem! We have premium loan clubs available at no extra cost.

We only provide use of premium golf balls.

Academy

We have 2 fully qualified PGA Professionals at North West Indoor Golf. There will always be a PGA Professional on site should you find yourself struggling with your game.

We use the latest technology including our Trackman 4 launch monitor and Boditrak pressure mat linked through high speed video to analyse the swing in precise detail.

We can also archive this data and share with you upon completion of your session, either as a refresher or so you can track your improvement.

Paul Woodcock (PGA Head Professional)
